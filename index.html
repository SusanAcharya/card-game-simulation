About Gangwars 2.0

Gangwars 2.0 is a strategic card battle simulation game. Players place their cards on a grid with various effects, utilizing different types of cards, debuffs, and event cards to influence the battle outcome. The game features an analytics system that allows players to explore alternative strategies and improve their performance.

In this demo, you must place 3 cards on the 3x3 grid. You can hover over the different cards and debuff cards to see their stats and influence. You can drag and drop the cards and debuff cards in any grid positions you want. Once all your 3 cards are placed, you can see the opponents cards and grid placement as well. For the purpose of testing, you can place your debuff cards after seeing the opponents cards. After that you can select any event to occur or you can leave it. Then you can start the battle and the battle log with display every activity that happens in each round. And the winner will be decided at the end!
